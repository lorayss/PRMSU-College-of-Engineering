Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering program focuses on the integration of hardware and software, preparing students to design and develop computer systems, networks, and embedded solutions for various industries.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering program provides knowledge in mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials, equipping students to design, analyze, and manufacture mechanical systems for diverse applications.

Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering program emphasizes infrastructure design and construction, focusing on sustainable development of buildings, roads, bridges, and water systems.

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering program develops expertise in electrical systems, power generation, and electronics, enabling graduates to contribute to industrial and renewable energy advancements.

Mining Engineering

The Mining Engineering program trains students in mineral exploration, extraction, and resource management, with an emphasis on environmental sustainability and safety practices.
